Hello!

I'm Matt, a front-end developer with a background in management and operations. My strengths include creative problem solving, team management, public speaking, and breaking down problems into small, manageable steps. I believe in staying evergreen, the concept of always striving to learn more and develop further and am always looking for new opportunities to put that idea into practice.

I also have a passion for cooking and food. I'll try any dish once, and more often than not a few more times after that as well. When I'm not cooking or coding I spend my time reading, camping, and exploring this big wonderful city in all its (sometimes) glory.
